Privacy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

365 Buttons doesn't collect anything. Your buttons are yours.

Data collection

None. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts, no telemetry.

Data storage

Your settings live on your device. The widget syncs with the app using Apple's App Groups. Nothing leaves your phone.

Third parties

There aren't any. No SDKs, no trackers, no ad networks.

Changes

If this ever changes, I'll update this page. But it won't. The whole point is that it doesn't want anything from you.
