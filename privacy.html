Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

The Short Version

Buttons does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Period.

Data Collection

We don't collect any data. No analytics. No tracking. No accounts. No telemetry.

Data Storage

All your settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and App Groups for widget sync). This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Buttons does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Widgets

The Buttons widget uses App Groups to share your display preferences between the main app and the widget. This data stays entirely on your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update this page. But honestly, our commitment to collecting zero data isn't going to change.
