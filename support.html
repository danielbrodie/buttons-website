Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add the widget to my home screen?

Long-press on your home screen, tap the + button in the top corner, search for "Buttons", and choose your preferred widget size.

What's the difference between Counting Up and Counting Down?

Counting Up shows buttons for days that have passed. Counting Down shows buttons for days remaining. Same data, different perspective—accumulation vs. urgency.

Can I track things other than the year?

Yes! Buttons supports multiple time scales: minutes in an hour, hours in a day, days in a week/month/year, weeks in a year, and even your lifetime in various units.

Why buttons?

Buttons are small, tangible, familiar. They mark presence and absence equally well. A jar of buttons is finite—you can hold it, count it, feel its weight change.

Does the app track my usage or collect data?

No. See our Privacy Policy. We collect nothing.
